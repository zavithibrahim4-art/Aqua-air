=====================================
AQUA AIR MONITOR
Application Overview
=====================================


1. INTRODUCTION
-------------------------------------

Aqua Air Monitor is a dashboard application designed to monitor
an Atmospheric Water Generator (AWG) called "Aqua Air".

An Atmospheric Water Generator is a device that collects moisture
from the air and converts it into clean drinking water. This app
helps users understand how much water is being produced, how the
device is performing, and what environmental conditions look like
at any given time.

The application presents all of this information in a visual,
easy-to-read format that anyone can understand at a glance.


2. PURPOSE OF THE APP
-------------------------------------

The purpose of Aqua Air Monitor is to bring together all the
important information about the water generation system into
one place.

With this app, users can:

  - See how much water has been produced today and over time
  - Check the current humidity and temperature levels
  - Monitor the quality of the water being generated
  - View the status of each device component
  - Receive alerts when the system needs attention
  - Get predictions for future water production

Instead of checking multiple sensors or instruments individually,
this app combines everything into a single, unified dashboard.


3. MAIN FEATURES
-------------------------------------

Water Production Tracking
  The app displays how much water the system has produced today
  and the total amount generated over its lifetime. Values are
  shown with clear numbers and visual indicators.

Humidity and Temperature Monitoring
  Since water generation depends heavily on air conditions, the
  app continuously displays the current humidity percentage and
  temperature. These values update automatically every few seconds.

Water Quality Monitoring
  The app tracks Total Dissolved Solids (TDS) and pH levels of
  the generated water. It shows a clear status indicator:
  Safe, Warning, or Unsafe, so users immediately know whether
  the water is safe to drink.

Device Status Display
  Each component of the AWG system is shown with a color-coded
  status: green for running, yellow for idle, and red for issues.
  Components include the Fan, Compressor, Condenser, UV Purifier,
  and Water Tank.

Maintenance Alerts
  The app provides notifications when maintenance is needed,
  such as filter replacements, tank drainage, or component
  overheating. Alerts are categorized by severity so the most
  urgent issues are highlighted first.

AI Water Prediction
  Using recent humidity trends and historical data, the app
  estimates how much water the system is likely to produce
  tomorrow. This helps users plan and anticipate output.

System Controls
  The app includes a simulated control panel where users can
  start or stop the system, adjust the target humidity threshold,
  and set the cooling level of the condenser.

Environmental Insights
  The app explains how current weather conditions affect water
  production. It shows the optimal humidity range and estimates
  the expected water output based on current conditions.


4. DASHBOARD OVERVIEW
-------------------------------------

The main dashboard is the first screen users see when they open
the app. It displays the most important information using:

  - Metric Cards: Large, easy-to-read cards showing key numbers
    like water produced, humidity, temperature, efficiency, and
    power consumption.

  - Live Monitoring Panel: A section that updates in real time,
    showing current readings for humidity, temperature, dew point,
    condenser temperature, airflow speed, and water collection rate.

  - Production Chart: A visual graph showing how much water was
    produced over the last 24 hours.

  - Prediction Card: A highlighted card showing the estimated
    water production for tomorrow.

  - Environmental Insights: A summary of current conditions and
    how they affect water generation.


5. PAGES IN THE APP
-------------------------------------

The application is organized into six main pages:

  Dashboard
    The home page with an overview of all key metrics, live
    monitoring data, production charts, and predictions.

  Water Analytics
    Detailed charts showing water production trends over
    different time periods: hourly, daily, and monthly.
    Also includes a graph comparing humidity levels to water
    output to show the relationship between them.

  Device Status
    Shows the status of each system component (Fan, Compressor,
    Condenser, UV Purifier, Water Tank). Includes a control
    panel for system operation and a visual water tank level
    indicator. Also displays the locations of deployed devices.

  Water Quality
    Monitors the safety of the generated water using gauges
    for TDS (Total Dissolved Solids) and pH levels. Shows an
    overall purity status and a historical chart of readings.

  Maintenance Logs
    Lists all active system alerts with severity levels.
    Displays warning banners for critical issues and maintains
    a log of past maintenance actions with dates and status.

  Settings
    Allows users to configure their preferences:
    - Choose between Liters and Gallons
    - Choose between Celsius and Fahrenheit
    - Enable or disable notification types
    - Adjust sensor calibration offsets


6. DATA DEMONSTRATION
-------------------------------------

This version of the Aqua Air Monitor uses simulated data to
demonstrate how a real monitoring system would work.

The simulated data includes:
  - Humidity readings (55% to 88%)
  - Temperature readings (22 to 36 degrees Celsius)
  - Water production rates
  - TDS values (45 to 130 ppm)
  - pH levels (6.7 to 7.6)

All values update automatically every 5 seconds to create a
realistic experience of watching a live monitoring system.

In a real deployment, this data would come from physical sensors
connected to the AWG device through a microcontroller (such as
an ESP32) communicating via WiFi.


7. DESIGN AND INTERFACE
-------------------------------------

The app uses a clean, modern design with a water-inspired color
theme. The main colors are deep blue, light aqua, and white,
creating a professional and calming visual experience.

Key design elements include:

  - Card-based layout for easy scanning of information
  - Color-coded status indicators (green, yellow, red)
  - Interactive charts and graphs for data visualization
  - Semi-circle gauges for water quality readings
  - Animated water tank level display
  - Responsive design that works on both desktop and mobile

The interface is designed so that even someone without technical
knowledge can quickly understand the system's performance.


8. CONCLUSION
-------------------------------------

The Aqua Air Monitor application demonstrates how modern
technology can be used to monitor and manage atmospheric
water generation systems effectively.

By presenting complex sensor data in a simple, visual format,
the app makes it easy for anyone to understand how much water
is being produced, whether the water is safe, and whether the
system is running properly.

This prototype shows the potential for real-world deployment
where such a dashboard would help operators maintain AWG
systems efficiently, reduce downtime, and ensure consistent
water quality.

The Aqua Air Monitor represents a practical application of
IoT (Internet of Things) technology in addressing water
scarcity through atmospheric water harvesting.
